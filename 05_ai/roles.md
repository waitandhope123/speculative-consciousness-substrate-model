# Roles and Responsibilities  
## Human and AI Participation in SCSM

This document summarizes the roles of participants involved in the creation
of the **Speculative Consciousness Substrate Model (SCSM)**.

---

## Human Author

- Originator of the model
- Sole decision-maker on content and structure
- Responsible for all claims and interpretations
- Accountable for errors and omissions

---

## ChatGPT

- Assisted with drafting, restructuring, and clarification
- Provided organizational suggestions under prompt
- Identified potential internal inconsistencies when asked
- No independent authority or authorship

---

## Claude

- Provided secondary review and refinement suggestions
- Assisted with tightening language and structure
- Offered alternative phrasings under human direction
- No independent authority or authorship

---

## Responsibility Boundary

All responsibility remains with the human author.
AI systems are tools, not collaborators in authorship.

---

## Final Note

Explicit role separation is maintained to ensure transparency, accountability,
and proper interpretation of this work.
